Lightweight Voice & Chat Tools Built for Frontline Teams

A Simple, Powerful Communication App for your agents

Set NenaCall is a lightweight communication application designed for frontline teams to handle both voice and chat interactions efficiently from a single interface. Built for speed and simplicity, NenaCall helps agents manage customer conversations without the complexity of traditional call center tools. 

Trusted Mini-App Among Many in NenaSasa Suite Apps

Why NenaCall? 

Traditional communication tools are often clunky, complex, and overloaded with features agents rarely use.  NenaCall removes this friction by delivering a clean, focused application for handling voice and chat conversations. 

With NenaCall, your teams focus less on operational friction and more on meaningful customer conversations.

Key Capabilities

Unified Voice & Chat Interface

Handle both voice calls and chat conversations from a single, easy-to-use agent interface.

Real-Time Chat Conversations

Centralized Conversation History
